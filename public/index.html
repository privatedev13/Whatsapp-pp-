How to Link Device

  1. Open WhatsApp on your primary phone.
  2. Go to Settings > Linked Devices.
  3. Tap Link a Device and select Link with phone number.
  4. Enter the Zaynix pairing code generated above.
  5. Follow the instructions to complete linking.
